About Us

Diana Sattarova also comes from a family of immigrants and is an immigrant herself. Currently being a high school student and going through a tough college process, she understands the struggle of many students and the factors that go into applying for and choosing a college. Aware of the problems many poorer communities go through, her and Mashzida want to make the transition to college for less advantaged students easier.

With a plan to decrease the federal interest rate for student loans and by decreasing interest rates, education will be available for more students. Coming from a very Russian household, Diana has many responsibilities at home along with the rigorous schoolwork of Stuyvesant High School. Students all around the US have many home responsibilities that can affect their education but should not bar them from being able to attend a good college where they are perfectly capable of doing well.
